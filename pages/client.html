Crane Data Monitoring

The primary objective of the Crane Monitoring Project was to implement a centralized SCADA-based system to continuously monitor crane operations. The system aimed to provide real-time visibility into crane status, operational hours, energy consumption, and fault diagnostics to enhance operational efficiency and decision-makingThe crane monitoring system has been successfully integrated with SCADA, enabling real-time tracking of crane operations, including runtime, stoppages, energy consumption, and fault diagnostics.

An automated reporting feature was implemented to send daily summary emails to customer representatives, providing detailed insights into crane performance. This execution has empowered the client to monitor production efficiency, identify operational bottlenecks, and take proactive maintenance actions based on fault alerts. As a result, customer benefits from improved transparency, reduced downtime, better energy management, and enhanced decision-making driven by accurate and timely data.

Shiftwise Production Data Transmission via E-mail

The purpose of this project was to automate the monitoring and reporting of weight meter data at customer site. The system captures real-time weight measurements and displays them on the HMI while also collecting relevant digital and analog input values. To ensure effective remote tracking, the system automatically sends shift-wise and daily reports via email to customer representatives at the main plant, located 60 km away from the mining area.

This automation project provides customer with real-time visibility of critical weight data directly on the HMI, along with digital and analog input status, improving local monitoring. Automatic shift-wise and daily email reports ensure that the main plant team stays updated without manual intervention, even from 60 km away. This enhances operational transparency, reduces dependency on manual reporting, and enables quicker decision-making. The system ultimately supports better coordination between the mining site and central plant, improves data accuracy, and enhances overall efficiency in logistics and material handling.
