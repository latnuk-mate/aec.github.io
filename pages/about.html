About Automatech Electric Co

At Automatech Electric Co, we specialize in the design, manufacturing, and assembly of high-quality electrical panels, tailored to meet the needs of industrial, commercial, and residential applications. With a commitment to safety, precision, and performance, we deliver reliable power distribution solutions that meet international standards and client expectations.

In addition to custom-built electrical panels, we offer a wide range of electrical accessories from trusted brands, ensuring our clients have access to everything they need for efficient and safe electrical installations. Whether you're a contractor, engineer, or facility manager, we provide expert guidance, dependable products, and technical support to power your projects with confidence.
